NaviSec Pentest Appliance

Getting started with getting your first remote internal assessment underway!

Download

To begin, simply download the remote pentest appliance here. Once downloaded, import it to your hypervisor!

Importing to your hypervisor

If you have a hypervisor such as VirtualBox, you can import it by navigating to File > Import Appliance, and then follow the setup wizard.

Note: you may get an error about the networking adapter not existing, to remedy this, go into the VM settings and set the networking adapter to bridged, preferrably on a connected ethernet interface

Sharing your Key

On the splash screen of the appliance, you will see a 6 word diceware key, keep this key very safe, and share it with the engineer responsible for your pentest.

Importing to VirtualBox

First, go to File -> Import Appliance

Next, Set the networking adapter to bridged

Then start it up!

Once the engineer responsible for your has imported your key, your appliance will reboot and connect to the NaviSec network!
